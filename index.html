ReplyKit

Save your replies once. Insert them anywhere you type — just type // and go.

What it does

ReplyKit lets you save your most-used replies and drop them into any text field on the web in one keystroke. Perfect for freelancers, customer support, online sellers, recruiters, and anyone who types the same things every day.

How it works

  1. Save your snippets from the toolbar popup.
  2. In any text field, type // or press Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Space.
  3. Pick a snippet — it's inserted instantly. Or use the Copy button to paste it manually.

Private by design

Your snippets are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared. Read the full Privacy Policy.
