ReplyKit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

ReplyKit is designed to be private by default.

What we collect

Nothing. ReplyKit does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Where your data lives

The text snippets you create are stored locally in your browser using the standard chrome.storage.local API. They never leave your device and are not sent to us or to any third party.

Permissions

  • Storage — to save your snippets locally in the browser.
  • Host access / activeTab — required so the extension can insert a selected snippet into the text field of the page you are actively using. ReplyKit only reads or writes text in a field when you explicitly trigger it (typing //, pressing the shortcut, or clicking a snippet). It does not read, log, or transmit page content.

Analytics & tracking

ReplyKit contains no analytics, no trackers, and no advertising.

Changes

If a future version (e.g. optional cloud sync or AI features) processes data outside your device, this policy will be updated and those features will be clearly opt-in.
